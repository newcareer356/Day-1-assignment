ROHIT GURUNANAK SHARMA

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian cricketer and the Vice Captain of team India in limited-over cricket. He plays for Mumbai in domestic cricket and captains Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League. He is a right-handed top-order batsman and an occasional right-arm off-break bowler. He is widely regarded as one of the best contemporary batsman in the world and one of the greatest opener of current era. Sharma was part of Indian squad which won the first ever T20I World Cup in 2007.

Rohit posted 264 off just 173 balls to set a new world record

Sharma made his Test debut at Eden Gardens in Kolkata against the West Indies and scored 177, the second-highest score on debut by an Indian.
